Risk & Mitigation Tracker

Operational discipline is part of the story.

Great projects do not just promise outcomes. They demonstrate that delivery risk is understood, monitored, and actively managed.

Risk Status Activated Mitigation
Inflation & equipment cost volatility Active Stage procurement, diversify funding channels, and prioritize resilient infrastructure categories.
Power instability High Deploy hybrid solar backup for Future-Labs and critical digital tools.
Political transition risk Managed Keep operations, assets, and reporting within project control rather than treasury structures.
Marketplace trust and adoption barriers Mitigated Use verification, local discovery, rating systems, and visible community anchoring.
Data credibility and reporting consistency Mitigated Standardize dashboards, quarterly reporting cycles, and indicator definitions.

What to embed later

Evidence, consistency, alignment to CSR priorities, and credible signals that the model can scale responsibly.
