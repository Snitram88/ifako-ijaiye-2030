The Crisis

The skills-opportunity mismatch is the real bottleneck.

Ifako-Ijaiye has scale, talent, and entrepreneurial energy, but too many residents remain disconnected from modern skills, digital visibility, and structured access to opportunity.

  • 38.5% youth unemployment undermines social and economic mobility
  • Digital literacy in public schools remains critically low
  • The informal economy dominates, but most businesses remain invisible online
  • Traditional interventions often lack accountability and measurable outcomes
The Cure

A practical pipeline from education to income.

The Accelerator creates a structured bridge from capability building to economic participation. It is designed to generate real momentum for youth, artisans, local government, and corporate partners.

  • Certify 1,200 youths annually in future-ready and green-collar tracks
  • Digitize and verify 500 MSMEs in the first 12 months
  • Track job placement, marketplace growth, and economic injection visibly
  • Protect delivery through a buffer governance model that reduces leakage risk
